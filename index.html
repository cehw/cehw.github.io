About

Current Role: Postdoctoral Researcher, HKUST Education: Ph.D. HKUST (2025) · BSc HHU (2021) Core Focus: Scientific ML for urban climate

Hi, I'm Han! 👋

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at HKUST, advised by Prof. Hui Su. My work lies at the intersection of AI and Earth Science. I specialize in developing scientific machine learning models, with a focus on spatiotemporal modeling and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), to tackle complex challenges in urban microclimate prediction and atmospheric dynamics.

I am deeply committed to the idea that AI will redefine how we conduct science and interact with our world. A lover of the great outdoors, I spend my free time hiking 🥾, practicing photography 📷, and staying connected to the sea 🌊.

Research Focus

Scientific ML

Physics-aware deep learning for robust prediction under limited observations and changing atmospheric conditions.

Urban Climate

Modeling canopy-layer temperature dynamics and the impact of urban form, vegetation, and synoptic forcing.

Spatiotemporal Forecasting

Multi-scale, graph-based, and multimodal methods for high-resolution city-scale nowcasting and short-term forecasting.
