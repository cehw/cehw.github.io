About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Civil and Environmental Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Hui Su. My work combines physical understanding with data-driven modeling for urban climate prediction.

I completed my Ph.D. at HKUST (advisor: Prof. Jiachuan Yang), focusing on graph neural networks and multimodal deep learning for short-term air temperature forecasting.

Research Focus

Scientific ML

Physics-aware deep learning for robust prediction under limited observations and changing atmospheric conditions.

Urban Climate

Modeling canopy-layer temperature dynamics and the impact of urban form, vegetation, and synoptic forcing.

Spatiotemporal Forecasting

Multi-scale, graph-based, and multimodal methods for high-resolution city-scale nowcasting and short-term forecasting.
